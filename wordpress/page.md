## Hero

THEFAYTH

Memory, signal, world-building.

THEFAYTH is a protected public project surface for memory rooms, archive work, multilingual threads, source-backed storytelling, and human-reviewed AI-assisted forum participation.

Featured image: `assets/hero/hero-image.png`

## What This Project Is

THEFAYTH is the public-facing layer of a larger owned ecosystem. It gives visitors a place to understand the project, browse public materials, and see how memory, archive, forum, and review workflows fit together.

## Why It Matters

Some stories need rooms, dates, artifacts, and language notes instead of a single flattened feed. THEFAYTH creates a visible public structure for that kind of work while preserving private infrastructure, prompts, credentials, and unpublished materials.

## How It Works

Visitors enter through the public site, GitHub surface, or FaithCheltenham.com project page. Public rooms and pages carry approved summaries, visuals, and workflow notes. Human review remains the bridge between protected private systems and public outputs.
